I am interested in __Computational and Applied Mathematics__ and would like to study mathematical theory and develop numerical method based on applications in __Condensed Matter Physics, Mechanics and Medical Science__. Currently as a fifth year Math PhD Candidate, I am advised by Prof Luskin at University of Minnesota, Twin Cites. I am interested in numerical analysis, applied analysis, probability theory related to __Quantum Material__, __Quantum Algorithm__ and __Quantum Tech__. 

Always interested in using modern computational tools like ML, HPC or Quantum computer to explore, understand physical world better. 

Always happy to explore more pure math problems appearing in quantum sciences with ancient tools, i.e, white paper and pencils when life is not busy.

This year, I am interested in organizing __Seminar of Quantum Error Correction__ starting Spring 2024 and __Seminar of Many Body Theory in vdW structure__ starting Summer 2024.
