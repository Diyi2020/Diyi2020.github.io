I am interested in __Computational and Applied Mathematics__ and would like to study mathematical theory and develop numerical method based on applications in __Condensed Matter Physics, Mechanics and Medical Science__. Currently as a fifth year Math PhD Candidate, I am advised by Prof Luskin at University of Minnesota, Twin Cites. I am interested in numerical analysis, applied analysis, probability theory related to __Quantum Material Science__, __Quantum Algorithm__ and __Quantum Tech__. 

Towards scientific machine learning, I am interested in applications within the scope of AI for Quantum Science.

Towards scientific computing/HPC, I am interested in improving numerical method and develop related analysis for Quantum material science. 

One of the most exciting direction, but maybe less doable direction, could be using quantum computer to explore the interesting electronic, mechanical properties of solids.

I am interested in holding __Quantum Error Correction Seminar__ in the near future. This connects to open quantum system, algebra, control and code.
