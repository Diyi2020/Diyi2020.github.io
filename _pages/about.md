I am interested in __Computational and Applied Mathematics__ and would like to study mathematical theory and develop numerical method based on applications in __Condensed Matter Physics, Mechanics and Medical Science__. Currently as a fifth year Math PhD Candidate, I am advised by Prof Luskin at University of Minnesota, Twin Cites. I am interested in numerical analysis, applied analysis, probability theory related to __Quantum Material Science__, __Quantum Algorithm__ and __Quantum Tech__. Always interested in using new computational tools like ML, HPC or Quantum computer to solve real world problems.

I am interested in holding __Quantum Error Correction Seminar__ in the near future. The goal is to start with basic QEC code and information theory in Spring 2024.
