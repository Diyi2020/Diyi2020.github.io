I am interested in Computational and Applied Mathematics and would like to actively explore applications involved in Quantum Science, Quantum Computing and Medical Science. How to use NISQ quantum computer for real world scenarios like simulating Quantum Material and Quantum Thermodynamics, performing Quantum Machine Learning and Drug design is of great interest to me.

Currently as a third year PhD Candidate, I am honored to be advised by Prof Luskin at University of Minnesota, Twin Cites. Most of my interests toward mathematics lie within modern analysis and probability theory, which are crucial foundation to numerical analysis, machine learning as well as quantum algorithms. Recently, I am mainly working on the scientific machine learning for 2D Material and Numerical analysis in Quantum computing.
