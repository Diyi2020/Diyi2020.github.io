Most of my interests toward mathematics lie within applying __modern analysis and probability theory__ to Numerical Analysis, Multi-Scale analysis and computation, Machine Learning. Recently, I am mainly working on the scientific machine learning for Moiré Material with emphasis on Operator Learning and Computation on NISQ Quantum Devices.

Exploring __Quantum Technology__, __Moiré Material__ are my main motivations for research in Quantum. On the other hand, with the advances of computation power, the aritificial intelligence and quantum computer start to influence __Scientific Computing__ , or computational mathematics. It would be fun to explore next generation scientific computing in the era with NISQ Quantum Computer, Supercomputers as well as early stage Artificial intelligence.
